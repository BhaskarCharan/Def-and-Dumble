Deaf Dumb

Sign gesture development

There have been several advancements in technology and a lot of research has been done to help the people who are deaf and dumb. Aiding the cause, Deep learning, and computer vision can be used too to make an impact on this cause. This can be very helpful for the deaf and dumb people in communicating with others as knowing sign language is not something that is common to all, moreover, this can be extended to creating automatic editors, where the person can easily write by just their hand gestures.

SIGN LANGUAGE DETECTION

What is sign language detection? Basically the concept of our idea is to understand the sign language of def and dumb people . There are millions of def and dumb in India and other countries who are still struggling to communicate to other normal people with the sign language. The application which we are going to develop converts sign language to normal English in voice and text and in turn takes our text or voice command to sign language. So that it will be helpful for both.
